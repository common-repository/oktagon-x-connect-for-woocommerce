Select pick-up points, print shipping labels, track and manage shipments from your dashboard for 100+ popular carriers available via supported TA-systems.

== Description ==
**Popular carriers, shipping methods & agent selectors**

Our connected carriers have 100+ connected carriers and is constantly adding new ones. You can easily set shipping methods, shipping methods & shipping rules in your settings just as usual.

**Built in Pick-up locations (Service Points)**
You can also give your customers the opportunity to choose a delivery location in your checkout by activating the agent selector when configuring a shipping method.

*All order & shipping management is handled directly in your admin dashboard.*

Features (more coming):

* Print shipping labels (single or bulk)
* Support 100+ popular carriers
* Shipping rules (by country, zipcode, weight, cartprice, free shipping over )
* Supports Coupons
* Book or store shipments (and print later)
* Edit shipping methods on orders incl. weight, package size etc.
* Order automation (by orderstatus)
* Track shipments
* Tracking link in e-mails
** Use your own carrier customer numbers or our partners **

== Installation ==

1. Upload "oktagon-x-connect-for-woocommerce.zip" and install.
1. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
1. Go to WooCommerce menu and click "X-Connect..." to start onboarding
1. Go to WooCommerce settings and setup shipping as usually, choose X-Connect as method
1. Add description, price, shipping methods, service point (if supported by method), prices vat or no vat, carrier logo and shipping rules.
1. Save and add more methods if needed.

= Is this plugin free? =
Yes, the plugin is free to use but you need to get a **free plugin license at [oktagon.se](https://oktagon.se)**

= Is Oktagon X-Connect free? =
No, you will have to pay the carriers (as usually) and also 2 SEK per order to the carrier. But that's it! * You can also use the partners carrier rates to get lower shipping rates *
